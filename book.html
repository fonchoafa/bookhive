Wings of Fire is the autobiography of A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, India's 11th president and a renowned aerospace scientist. The book traces his journey from a small town in Tamil Nadu to becoming one of India's most beloved figures. A story of determination, vision, and the power of dreaming big against all odds.
